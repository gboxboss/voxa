Refund Policy

Introduction. At Optimuu, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction. In the rare event of misuse or abuse, this Refund Policy protects our customers. This policy applies to purchases made from the Effective Date onward.

Refund Eligibility
Customers may request a refund for purchases made through the Optimuu platform. Optimuu will determine, at its sole discretion, if misuse or abuse has occurred.

Exclusions
The following situations are excluded under this policy:

  • Requesting a refund more than 30 days after purchase.
  • Requesting a refund for purchases made outside the Optimuu platform.
  • Requesting a refund for services that have been fully rendered or digital goods that have already been downloaded.
  • Submitting excessive or duplicate claims.

Filing a Claim
To file a claim under this policy, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You must remain the owner of the payment method used for the purchase.
  • You must not have contributed to or induced misuse or abuse in any way.
  • You must fully complete the refund request form and comply with any subsequent information requests.

Modification or Termination
Optimuu reserves the right to modify or terminate this policy at any time, in its sole and absolute discretion, without prior notice.

Contact
If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at contact@gosupportt.space.
